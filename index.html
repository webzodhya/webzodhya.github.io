Have A Question?

Frequently
Asked Questions

  • Q.Does the Saver device tamper with the warranty of the HVAC system?

    No, it doesn't tamper with any warranties. Our device sits externally in series with the power supply. We neither meddle with any of the internal circuits nor with the existing infrastructure of the space.

  • Q.Does the Saver switch on & off the AC? Is it like an automation device?

    No, the Saver is neither an automation device nor does it take control over the AC. The complete control is still with the user. Our device will only find the least energy consuming path using its self-learning algorithms to reach the set conditions.

  • Q.Is the Saver like a capacitor/power factor correction/ voltage regulator?

    None of the above. Our device works on a sophisticated self-learning algorithm which understands the user behavior and conditions to consume the least possible energy.

  • Q.How much does the Saver cost?

    The pricing is a subscription model based on the energy bill and savings possible. We also offer one-time payment based on the client profile & buildings.
